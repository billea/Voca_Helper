Vocabulary List

1. Ameliorate - to make better or improve
   Example: The new policy will ameliorate working conditions.

2. Capitulate - to surrender or give up
   Example: The army was forced to capitulate after the siege.

3. Deleterious - harmful or damaging
   Example: The deleterious effects of pollution are well documented.

4. Efficacious: effective in producing a desired result
   The medicine proved efficacious against the infection.

5. Gregarious
   Sociable and enjoying the company of others
   She has always been a gregarious person who loves parties.
